Inspiration

Even as a kid, I’ve always been told that I’ve had a curious mind and it’s always been my greatest asset. I use the question “Why?” as my vehicle to explore my intrinsic curiosity, and I’ve been able to do this with the combination of my majors: understand how we can make processes be more efficient, why we feel and think the ways we do, and discover patterns that lie within data. My curiosity serves as my motivation to continue to continuously adapt, grow, and develop new ways outside of my comfort zone. It is my guiding principle in this world to continue to challenge myself and simply remove complacency from my life. Will's Website
